Our Mission
America Has Done This Before. This Time You Can Be Prepared.

ICE Ready was built because fear should never be the reason someone doesn't know their rights. The founder is Colombian-American, personally connected to the communities most at risk, and building this at the exact moment it matters most.

The app is free. It will stay free. No ads. No data selling. No paywall between a family and their rights. Built by iCyberAI LLC, Wyoming.
