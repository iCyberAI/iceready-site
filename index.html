Our Mission
America Has Done This Before. This Time You Can Be Prepared.

ICE Ready was built on a simple conviction: fear should never be the reason someone doesn't know their rights. The Constitution doesn't ask where you were born — due process and equal protection belong to everyone. And when those rights can be stripped from one community, no one's rights are safe.

This app is for anyone the system is failing — the profiled, the detained, the families answering a knock at the door. It is free and it will stay free. No ads. No data selling. No paywall between a family and their rights. Built by iCyberAI LLC, Wyoming.
